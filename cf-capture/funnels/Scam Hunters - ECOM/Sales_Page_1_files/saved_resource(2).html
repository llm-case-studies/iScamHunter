How Does it Work?


Its surprisingly simple.
No "spy games." No danger. No heroics.

At Scam Hunters, we don't infiltrate.
We just show up and stay alert.

Take the case we documented at Crypto-Fakes.com, where scammers tried to impersonate the real company Mobomo.

They set up a fake site mobomo.xyz and started recruiting "students" for supposed job training.
We signed up like anyone else would. No hacking. No tricks.
Just a little patience and a lot of playing along.

By acting like distracted, slow-moving trainees, we stayed close enough to observe what was really going on without rushing into their traps.

Meanwhile, with just basic research simple WHOIS lookups, checking domains, reading between the lines we uncovered what they were doing.

When the first fake site collapsed, most people dropped off.
We lazily shuffled along with the "class" to the next domain watching, recording, and documenting.

The result?
They couldnt hold their operation together.
They kept losing ground, moving faster, becoming more exposed.
Eventually, they gave up impersonating Mobomo altogether.

Theres no way to catch scammers one by one.
But when they start seeing shadows in every group...
When they can't tell if their next "student" is a Scam Hunter...
When they have to run before they collect a dime...

The game ends.

Thats the real power of community.

Not just exposing scams
But making it almost impossible for a Scam Hunter to be tricked ever again.
